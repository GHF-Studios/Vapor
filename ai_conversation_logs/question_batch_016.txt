Question Batch 016: Vapor AGENT.md Structure

1. Should `Vapor/AGENT.md` be plain Markdown, but written more like a compact operating protocol than normal documentation?
A: Yes.

2. Should it have only a few top-level sections, maybe `Purpose`, `Authority`, `Default Mode`, `Approval Rules`, `Repo Boundaries`, `Dependency Rules`, `Docs Rules`, and `Question Batches`?
A: Yes, but be mindful of pre-emptive definition of categories or sections or organizational buckets of any kind, yk? But, fundamentally, yes.

3. Should `Purpose` explicitly say Vapor is the root repo of the ecosystem, while avoiding product doctrine details?
A: Oh no, product doctrine/stack details are very much (somewhat at least) relevant here I think.

4. Should `Authority` say owner answers in chat are high-authority raw input, but not automatically doctrine until integrated?
A: Yes.

5. Should `Default Mode` say: ask questions first, prefer question batches for broad uncertainty, and do not rush synthesis?
A: Yes.

6. Should `Approval Rules` explicitly include no formatting, no tests, no validation, and no file creation without approval?
A: Yes.

7. Should `Repo Boundaries` name the current four repos and dependency direction?
A: Yes.

8. Should `Repo Boundaries` forbid Vapor from importing/naming Spacetime Engine or Loo Cast internals, but allow generic examples like "engine" and "game"?
A: Yes, exactly. The roles of an engine and a game and mods for those and extension mods and all that other shit, yes, that's clearly part of vapor, but the concrete *instances* of these concepts are NOT, yk?

9. Should `Dependency Rules` distinguish development dependencies, release dependencies, and distributed content/artifact dependencies?
A: Yes?

10. Should `Docs Rules` explicitly ban `/docs` by default while allowing `ai_conversation_logs/` as a special raw-history folder?
A: Yes.

11. Should `Question Batches` specify the original format: title, optional note/context, numbered questions, `A:` slots, raw owner answers?
A: Yes.

12. Should `Question Batches` say one batch per file and preserve numbering from the wider project history?
A: Yes.

13. Should it include a short "what this unlocks" convention for chat batches, even if the saved raw logs may omit those summaries?
A: Yes.
